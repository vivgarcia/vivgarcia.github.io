Overview

The Parks & Recreation trivia game was created using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.

How to play:
  • Press the 'Start Game' button to begin
  • You have 45 seconds to answer 5 questions
  • Once you answer all 5 questions, your score will be tallied
  • Click to 'Reset' button to play again
