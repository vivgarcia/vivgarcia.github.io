Overview

Crystal Collector was created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

How to play:
  • You will be given random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals. By clickin on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • Win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of the crystals is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Everytime the game restarts, the game will change the value of the crystals.
