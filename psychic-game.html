Overview

The Psychic game was created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

How to play:
  • Press a letter on your keyboard between A - Z
  • You have 10 guesses
  • Your guesses will display on the page
  • If you guess correctly, an alert will notify you and the game will reset.
  • If you guess wrong, there will be an alert and the game will reset
